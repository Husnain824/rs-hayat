About

Serving with Dedication

As the Provincial Minister for School Education in Punjab, Rana Sikandar Hayat brings a vision of transformative education reform that prioritizes accessibility, quality, and innovation across the province.

Representing PP-183 Kasur-IX (Phool Nagar), he has consistently demonstrated commitment to public service and community development. His leadership in the education sector focuses on modernizing infrastructure, improving teacher training, and ensuring every child has access to quality education.

A proud member of Pakistan Muslim League (N), his work reflects the party's dedication to national progress and educational excellence.

Educational Reform

Leading comprehensive initiatives to modernize Punjab's education system, with focus on digital learning, infrastructure development, and teacher empowerment.

Community Service

Deep roots in Kasur-IX constituency, working closely with local communities to address educational needs and development challenges.

Innovation & Progress

Championing technology integration in classrooms and promoting scholarship programs to support talented students across Punjab.

Recent Initiatives

Making Impact

Honhaar Scholarship Program Launch

Addressing the ceremony at University of Veterinary and Animal Sciences (UVAS) Lahore, announcing scholarships and laptop distribution for DVM students. This initiative represents the government's commitment to empowering the next generation of professionals through education and technology access. The Honhaar program aims to support talented students from across Punjab, ensuring that financial constraints do not hinder academic excellence.
